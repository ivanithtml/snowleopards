Issyk-Kul

Lake Issyk-Kul is the pearl of Kyrgyzstan, known for its clear waters and picturesque landscapes. Thanks to the salinity of the water and warm winters, the lake does not freeze, which makes it a great place for year-round recreation. The lake is surrounded by hundreds of mountain peaks, which attracts lovers of tourism, trekking and mountaineering.

Son-Kul

Lake Son-Kul is a large high-altitude lake, the second largest in Kyrgyzstan. It is located in Naryn oblast, about 280 kilometers from Bishkek. Son-Kul is located at an altitude of 3000 meters in a large intermountain valley bounded by the Sonkul-Too Range to the north and the Moldo-Too Range to the south. This is a unique place where you can enjoy clean water, picturesque landscapes and rich nature!
